The Hobby

My brother introduced me into playing video games when I was pretty young. Being that I looked up to him, it's easy to see why I ended up liking video games too. It first started with the Nintendo 64 and has now progressed all the way to the Xbox One. It's something I like to do in my free time and will always try to get my friends to play with as it's always a good time to play games with them. On this site, you can observe my current collection of video games.
